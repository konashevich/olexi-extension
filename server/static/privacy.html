Privacy Policy

Effective: 14 August 2025

The OLEXI Extension does not collect or store your personal data, browsing history, prompts, or research results. All interactions stay on your device unless you direct a request to your configured Olexi Host service.

Data flow

  • The extension UI sends your prompt to an Olexi Host you control (default: http://127.0.0.1:3000 or your deployed host).
  • The host may call third‑party services to fulfil the request:
    • Google Gemini (if configured) for planning/summarisation.
    • AustLII (austlii.edu.au) for legal search results.

Third‑party services

We do not control nor are we responsible for what is collected by third‑party services, including Google Gemini and AustLII (austlii.edu.au). Their processing is subject to their own terms and privacy policies.

Local storage

The extension may store your API key locally in chrome.storage.local or localStorage for convenience. This information does not leave your device.

Telemetry and logging

The extension does not send analytics or telemetry. If you deploy the Olexi Host, it may keep minimal operational logs for rate limiting and security; that configuration is outside the extension and under your control.

Contact

For questions, please open an issue in the project’s GitHub repository or contact the maintainer via the Chrome Web Store listing email.

Olexi AI is an independent project and is not affiliated with or endorsed by AustLII.
