Privacy Policy

Effective: 18 August 2025

Overview

The Olexi AI Extension adds an assistant panel on supported legal websites. It does not collect personal information, sell data, or use data for advertising or profiling.

Data processed to provide the service

When you submit a prompt in the extension, the prompt and minimal context necessary to perform the request are sent to your configured Olexi Host service to generate search results and summaries. The extension itself does not retain this data.

What we store locally

  • Minimal UI settings only (no API keys). Storage is local to your device and is not synced or shared.

What we do not collect

  • No analytics or telemetry from the extension.
  • No sale of data and no advertising use.
  • No collection of your browsing history by the extension.

Retention

The extension does not retain prompts or results. Your chosen Olexi Host may keep minimal operational logs (for example, error or rate‑limit logs) depending on its configuration.

Your choices

  • You can clear stored data by removing the extension’s data or uninstalling the extension.
  • You may configure or change the Olexi Host service you use.

Contact

For questions, please contact the maintainer via the Chrome Web Store listing or the project repository.
